Synopsis

Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy who sells charcoal for a living, finds his family slaughtered by demons. His sister Nezuko is the only survivor, but she has been transformed into a demon herself. Determined to find a cure for her and avenge his family, Tanjiro becomes a Demon Slayer.
